Forex Explained

  • Forex is not Foreign Exchange of currencies.
  • Forex is speculation between you and the broker.
    • You loose = broker gains.
    • You win = broker looses.

      • Broker are very rich! 99% trader do not make money.

  • Forex is hard and it is meant to be hard(nothing is easy).
  • You won't become rich but can make some cash.

Rules To Be Followed

  • Trade on High effect news events only(Discipline)
  • Do not Trade when there are many high effect events for a single currency.
  • Couple two strong and weak interest rate currency before news.
  • Analyze the news understand its effect whether to buy or sell.
  • More leverage less risk
